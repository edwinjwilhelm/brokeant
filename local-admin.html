Become a Local Admin

Help launch BrokeAnt in your town and keep listings clean and useful for your community.

Limited Admin Access

What you can do

  • Approve or reject listings for your town only
  • Flag spam or inappropriate images
  • Keep the marketplace clean for your area

What you cannot access

  • No access to payments or financial data
  • No access to private user data
  • No control outside your town

Perks for Local Admins

  • Local Admin badge on your listings
  • Extra advertisement credits for your town
  • Featured placement for your town page (early launch)

How to apply

Email us at sales@brokeant.com with:

  • Your town name
  • Your BrokeAnt account email
  • Why you want to help run your town page

We usually respond within 1-2 business days.
